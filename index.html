About Us

  

We are the stuff of Parisian dreams, with chandelier-clad dining rooms, foliage-filled courtyards and elegant rooms, each decorated with crown molding and pops of bright pastels. We are a French Boutique Hotel that is geared towards girls that studying abroad in France and want a little getaway from the weekend in the city of love. Hotel built in 1932, Last partial renovation 2008, Number of floors 4, Total number of rooms 12, Number of single rooms 12, Number of double rooms 18, Reception manned 24 hours a day, Earliest check-in 14:00, Latest check-out 12:00, Multilingual staff on hand 24 hours a day, English and French speaking staff available during the day, English-speaking staff available at night, Number of luxury rooms 30.
