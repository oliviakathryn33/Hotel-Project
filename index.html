About Us

  

We are a French Boutique Hotel that is geared towards girls that studying abroad in France and want a little getaway from the weekend in the city of love. Hotel built in 1932, Last partial renovation 2008, Number of floors 4, Total number of rooms 12, Number of single rooms 4, Number of double rooms 8, Reception manned 24 hours a day, Earliest check-in 14:00, Latest check-out 12:00, Multilingual staff on hand 24 hours a day, English-speaking staff available during the day, English-speaking staff available at night, Number of luxury rooms 30.

Booking

Our Address is 40, Rue du Faubourg St. Honoré, 75008 Paris, France. You can reach us at the following link: Faisdebeauxrêves.info. Situated in the heart of Montparnasse, the hotel is centrally located close to Saint-Germain-des-Près and Luxembourg Gardens. The famous Eiffel Tower can be reached within 15 minutes via public transportation. Orly International Airport is located 14 kilometres away.
